Data Sources

Our data will come from respected sources such as the International Energy Agency (IEA) and national electricity grids. Using data on electricity consumption, energy efficiency metrics, and renewable energy adoption, we aim to create an accurate analysis of electricity usage patterns and their environmental impacts.

Analytical Tools

We will use advanced tools like smart grids and energy monitoring systems to analyze and optimize electricity usage. Energy storage solutions and renewable energy integration will also be key components of our approach to sustainable electricity management.
